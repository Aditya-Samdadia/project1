Earth after some years

Environ is a platform where one can be aware 'bout the environment and can also share facts, spread awareness and promote saving the environment.

All you have to do to share is register (it's free). Then you can conveniently go to the share option and share to your heart's content. To ultimately contribute in saving the world from it's almost inevitable future.

This site aims to spread awareness about the environment, to spread ideas that will lead a individual to live a life that is least damaging to the environment, for people to be conscious about their immediate surroundings. Although all of this feels irrelevant or un-important, there will be a time when people will regret not caring about the Earth, our one and only home.

It is not about not using our resourses at all but about using them smartly, in such a way that they are least or even 0% damaging to the environment.

Most people think that to make the Earth pollution free, they will have to invest money, time, or the luxuries or all of the above; and most of the time thats the case. But what if we work more smartly, what if we only invest by making ourselves aware, to motivate the people who do research about these technologies and let them do the hard-work. This way we won't have to invest in money (maybe a little), nor in time as the reasearchers are the ones doing th work and neither in luxuries because the researchers will surely develop newer and better technology in terms of utility as well as pollutibility.
